This site practices what the catalog preaches.

## What we collect: as close to nothing as possible

- **No cookies.** The site sets none. There is no consent banner because there is
  nothing to consent to.
- **No third-party trackers.** No Google Analytics, no Meta pixel, no ad SDKs —
  and the [enforcement engine](/policies/enforcement/) would block them if a
  contributor tried to add one.
- **No account, no login** to read anything.

## Analytics, if any

If the maintainer enables analytics, it is **Plausible** — a cookieless,
privacy-respecting analytics tool that collects no personal data and no
cross-site identifiers. It is only loaded when a `PLAUSIBLE_DOMAIN` is configured
at build time; in local development and in forks, **no analytics script is
included at all**. You can confirm this in `astro.config.mjs`.

<Aside type="tip" title="Verify it yourself">
Open your browser's network tab on any page. With analytics disabled you should
see requests only to this site's own origin. That's the same kind of
network-observation check the [recipes](/recipes/) ask you to run on what *you*
build.
</Aside>

## Search

Site search is powered by **Pagefind**, which builds a static index at deploy
time and runs entirely in your browser. Your search queries never leave your
device and hit no server.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell, share, or broker data — there isn't any to broker.
- We don't embed fonts, scripts, or images from third-party CDNs that could log
  your IP. The site uses your system font stack and self-hosts its assets.
- We don't route anything through Meta, OpenAI, or xAI. That's the whole point.

## Contact

Privacy questions or concerns: open an issue on the
[repository](https://github.com/martinmontero/wecanjustbuildthings.dev), or see
[Security](/security/) for how to report a vulnerability privately.
