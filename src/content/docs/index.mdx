## For the people who build to protect their communities

An organizer who needs to document evictions without exposing tenants. A
collective building a tool their members can actually trust. A nonprofit shipping
something real with a tiny team. You bring the intent and the knowledge of what
your community needs — an AI agent and this project bring everything else, so what
you ship is indistinguishable from the work of a professional team.

**A harness like Goose or Claude Code gives the agent hands. This gives it
judgment:** a verified catalog of freedom-tech tools, a guided flow from idea to
shipped, reusable agent skills, and safeguards that have your back — so when the
agent runs, it builds on decisions already made, verified, and documented.

## Built for you, not for the platform

This is **not** built for the founder who already owns the agent network and sets
the norms. It's built for the organizer, the nonprofit, the collective — the
people who have to *trust* a system without being able to see inside it, and who
deserve to make the calls about what they depend on with their eyes open. The
safeguards exist to serve them, never to fence them in.
