## A note on statistics

The cookbook reports on cohorts and outcomes. The analog here is the catalog's
own health: how many entries are `verified` vs. `under_review`, and how fresh the
license and maintenance data is. Those numbers are produced by the weekly
automation described in [About & maintenance](/about/), not asserted by hand.
