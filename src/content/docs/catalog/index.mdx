A thousand-plus building blocks for the tools your community needs — the same ones
the And Other Stuff crew ships freedom tech with. Each is **license-checked at a
commit** and screened so it can't quietly betray the people you build for (the
[receipts](/policies/enforcement/) are on every entry). Pick with confidence:
**search (top of the page) is the fastest way to find a specific tool**, and the
sections below surface the most-used entries per area.

<Aside type="note" title="Reading the badges">
**verification** — `verified` (license pinned to a commit), `under_review`
(declared but not yet machine-confirmed at a commit — often needs a token pass),
or `blocked`. **status** — `active` (&lt;90d), `minimal` (&lt;12mo), `dormant`
(&lt;36mo), `abandoned`. Lists are sorted by how many And Other Stuff repos use
the tool.
</Aside>
