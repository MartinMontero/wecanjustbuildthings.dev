Every entry here is **license-checked at a specific commit** and screened against
the [exclusion policy](/policies/). Each shows its verification and maintenance
status so you can decide with your eyes open.

<Aside type="note" title="Reading the badges">
**verification** — `verified` (license pinned to a commit), `under_review`
(pending a maintainer/token pass), or `blocked`. **status** — `active` (< 90d),
`minimal` (< 12mo), `dormant` (< 36mo), `abandoned`.
</Aside>
